Admin

ADMIN 2 IS IN THE WORKS!!

Admin had been enjoying an unexpected late success here in Itch.io. I was surprised to find this out after years of its release, and I FINALLY took the time to fix the game music which was broken because of Godot shenanigans (thanks 3p0ch for the help). After some consideration, I decided I want to make a sequel (or rather the "full" game) to Admin! I still have the sketches of other rooms I wanted to included in the original game, but didn't have time to do so. I'll working on that, little by little and on my own. Unfortunately I have a day job and many other things to do with my life, so any kind of incentive - be it money or nice words -would be appreciated.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

It’s a fresh new year and you have a fresh new job! You thought being an IT guy would be easy, but your boss gives you a huge challenge right on the first day. Turns out that, after the new year, everyone in the office seems to have forgotten their computer’s password! Now you have to play the detective and figure out how to unlock the computers in the office.

Click the "?" for a hint, you get a new hint every minute.

This game was made for "My First Game Jam: Winter 2021" which had the theme "Locked"

Comments:

The game, in itself, has the same gameplay each time (unlocking a PC by finding objects, post-its...) but it's still fun to do.

I wonder if the game creators have considered making something longer, with more characters, more floors? Maybe even a larger room with doors that require codes to unlock, for example?

The office setting reminds me a lot of the game The Stanley Parable —perhaps you could draw inspiration from that type of gameplay for a project (what would happen if we didn’t follow the game’s intended path?).

No bugs were detected, and the game is quite intuitive. Overall, I would rate the difficulty around 4/10 (10/10 being impossible, and 5/10 being where some thinking is required).

Too bad there are only six levels for now!
